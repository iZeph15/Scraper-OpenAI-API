WALKER GROUP

ABOUT US

Walker Group Ventures stays on top by investing
in their most valuable resource: their people.

In 1994, Walker Group Ventures took over a small collision repair shop in Vancouver, British Columbia. Not long after this, brothers Scott and Robert Walker started shifting their skills in the shop to a direction that aligned with their passion: luxury vehicles. What started as a single facility has now evolved into No. 1 Collision Group and Mercedes-Benz Collision, which has five locations in the Greater Vancouver Area and one in Bellingham, Washington.
