Your success is our priority

The ports of Tacoma and Seattle have formed The Northwest Seaport Alliance to jointly manage marine cargo operations to strengthen the Puget Sound gateway.

Our strategic location in the northwest corner of the U.S. and focus on efficiency, reliability and customer service help make us the easiest and best place for moving your cargo.
