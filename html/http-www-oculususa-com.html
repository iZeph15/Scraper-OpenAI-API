08/25/2021 Myopia Master® Receives FDA Clearance

The new OCULUS Myopia Master® is the world’s first device to combine the important measurement metho... more

The new OCULUS Myopia Master® is the world’s first device to combine the important measurement methods of axial length, refraction values, and the central corneal radii. Featuring an intuitive alignment mechanism coupled with auto-capture, the Myopia Master® quickly provides results for expedited practice flow and enhanced patient care.


"I am excited to see OCULUS's FDA clearance in the United States for the Myopia Master. The fast acquisition of axial length, autorefraction, and K's will greatly benefit patient management." David Kading, OD - The Myopia Podcast
