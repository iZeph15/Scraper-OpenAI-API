CASE STUDY

Improving Reliability While
Saving Ratepayers $800 Million

Leading investor-owned utility’s highly successful cable rejuvenation program helped reverse a rise in failures that began in the early 1990s. Since then, cable rejuvenation has helped the utility deliver reliable electric power to one of the fastest growing state in America, addressing more cable, in less time, with less impact to ratepayers than replacement.

Rejuvenation Program:

  • Rejuvenated more than 12 million feet of their URD cable.

  • Resulted in an annual reduction of 1,300 outages vs. the predictive model on which they based the need program.

  • Deferred the need for replacement by 22 years.

  • Saved their rate payers over $800 million.
