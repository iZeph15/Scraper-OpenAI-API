A HISTORY OF QUALITY

While the industry has changed since Beall’s beginning over 100 years ago, the drive for businesses to improve their operations, save money and do more with less remains constant. Truckers throughout the western United States and Canada are proud to pull Beall trailers to help them do just that. Our legendary reputation was earned through a commitment to stringent design specifications, manufacturing excellence, and superior styling. The result: trailers known for an unprecedented balance between premier craftsmanship and reliable performance.
