Covestic awarded Most Promising ServiceNow Solutions Provider from CIOReview

Covestic, a ServiceNow Elite Partner recently received the award of Most Promising ServiceNow Solutions Provider from CIOReview, a leading print and digital magazine that bridges the gap between enterprise technology vendors and buyers.

According to Scott Jacocks, VP of Service Management Solutions at Covestic, Covestics secret sauce is our iNow methodology and tools which combine a cloud-based business process management solution with the ServiceNow Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM) suite to comprehensively run the design and implementation phases.

With iNow, Covestics customers have full transparency into every aspect of the project to include achievement-to-plan, risks/issues/actions, changes and budget posture. This means surprises are rare and delivery-to-scope, schedule and budget are the norm.
