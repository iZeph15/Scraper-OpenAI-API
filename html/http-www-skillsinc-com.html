A Thriving Business With A Social Mission

Skills Inc. is a self-supporting, nonprofit social enterprise and leading aerospace supplier that demonstrates the power of an inclusive workplace by producing quality products in advanced manufacturing while utilizing best practices associated with disability employment.

Skills Inc. has ONE mission, to create and maintain a stable, rewarding place of work, where persons with disabilities can contribute their skills. Our success comes as a result of a balanced connection between our TWO bottom lines, financial and social. We accomplish our mission in THREE ways, by employing people with disabilities, vocational programs, and by serving as a role model and resource to the community.
